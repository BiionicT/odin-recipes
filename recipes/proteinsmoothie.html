Protein Smoothie

Description

Are you trying to get ripped? This wont do it, sorry, but it can help!

Enjoy one of these bad boys Daily (if taking before bed I prefer to substitute casein for the whey

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Frozen Fruit (your choice, I prefer strawberry, mango, pineapple and blueberry)
  • 1 Scoop Whey Protein Powder (unflavored of vanilla preferred)
  • 8oz Orange Juice
  • Blender
Steps
  1. Add frozen fruit to blender receptacle
  2. Scoop protein powder on top of fruit
  3. Pour orange juice on top of fruit and protein
  4. Blend smoothie until smooth consistency (add more orange juice or fruit if needed)
  5. Pour into cup
  6. Enjoy!
