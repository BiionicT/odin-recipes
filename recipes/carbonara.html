Lasagna

Description

MAMA MIA! A nice simple carbonara recipe. This one will remind you of home and can be made with just a few ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • 2oz Parmesan
  • Pasta (Spaghetti preferred)
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 4oz Bacon
Steps
  1. Salt and Boil Water
  2. Dice Bacon into small strips
  3. Grate 2oz Parmesan
  4. Whisk together 4 Egg Yolks and 2 Wole Eggs then add 3/4 of Parmesan
  5. Cook Bacon until crispy and set aside, save 3tbsp fat
  6. Cook Pasta a few minutes short of instruction and save 1 3/4 cup pasta water(it will cook the rest of the way later)
  7. Add 1 cup of pasta water into pan used to cook bacon and bring to boil, add pasta
  8. Cook while stirring the pasta until al dente and remove from heat
  9. Whisk 1/4 Cup pasta water into egg and cheese mixture and slowly pour into pasta while stirring
  10. Stir until creamy texture covers the pasta adding in pasta water as needed
  11. Stir in Bacon and plate
  12. Top pasta with black pepper and parmesan
  13. Enjoy!
