Lasagna

Description

MAMA MIA! No spicy meatball here but I got a nice lasagna recipe for you. This is a classic, we got cheese, meat, red sauce and pasta!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodle
  • Ground Beef
  • Mozzarella
  • Onion
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Red Sauce
Steps
  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Dice Onion and cook Ground Beef until browned. Set aside
  3. Boil Water and cook lasagna noodles as intructions advise
  4. Begin Layering Lasagna (Noodle, followed by ricotta, then meat, then sauce)
  5. On last layer place only sauce and mozarrella on the top
  6. Place Lasagna in Oven
  7. Cook until cheese is bubbling
  8. Remove from oven and allow to cool a few minutes
  9. Enjoy!
